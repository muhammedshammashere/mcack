Calligraphy Art Centre Kerala

  • Dedicated to preserving and promoting the sacred art of Arabic calligraphy.
  • Aims to elevate Kerala's students to an international level in traditional Arabic calligraphy.
  • Offers a wide range of courses, including Arabic Calligraphy, Islamic Illumination, Geometric Patterns, and more.

Welcome to the newly inaugurated Calligraphy & Art Institute, nestled under the auspices of Madin Academy in the vibrant city of Malappuram. Steeped in tradition and dedicated to the preservation and promotion of the sacred art of Arabic calligraphy. As we open our doors to enthusiasts, learners, and connoisseurs alike, we extend a warm invitation to embark on a journey that transcends cultural boundaries. Beyond the elegance of Arabic script, our curriculum extends its reach to encompass English and Malayalam calligraphy, Islamic llumination, embroidery, ceramic art, and more. Our commitment extends beyond teaching the technicalities of calligraphy; it encompasses workshops, exhibitions, competitions and interactive sessions by world renowned masters. We invite you to join us in unraveling the layers of cultural richness embedded in each creation. The institute stands not just as a place of learning but as a cultural hub, fostering a community that appreciates the timeless beauty of Arabic calligraphy and its manifold connections to diverse art traditions.

Why Choose MCACK

We are dedicated to the exquisite art of calligraphy, fostering a space where enthusiasts and artists converge to explore and celebrate the diverse forms of this intricate writing style. With a focus on preserving traditions and fostering creativity, the center serves as a vibrant platform for the promotion and cultivation of calligraphic expression in the culturally rich region of Kerala.

People You Should To Know

Sayyid Ibraheemul Khaleel Al Bukhari is one of the India’s most recognized Muslim personalities. He has traveled the world, lectured to thousands, and composed a number of works spanning education and contemporary issues. He was born in 22nd February 1964 in Kozhikkode district of Kerala State, India in a family of esteemed scholars.

Sayyid Ibraheemul Khaleel Al Bukhari

Founder and Chairman &at Ma'din Academy

Arabic calligraphy is not a way of writing as we like or copying someone else’s work. This is a common misconception across Kerala. It is important for everyone to understand that just like learning in a madrasa, school or through formal lessons, one should first learn from a qualified teacher who has received the proper certification (ijazah) for teaching. Recognizing this need, Ma’din Academy of Kerala established Ma’din Calligraphy & Art Centre, Kerala (MCACK) in January 2024 to teach traditional calligraphy.

Anfas Wandoor

Director & Calligraphy Master, MCACK

Arabic calligraphy is worship for Muqtar Ahmed, an Indian calligrapher who has made a mark for himself at a global level. Hailing from a remote village in Telangana, and currently based in Bengaluru, Muqtar is on a mission to revive this dying art in India.

Muqtar Ahmed

Calligrapher at IIIAC Bangalore

He is a Calligrapher, illustrator and artist based in Keralam. For over four decades he has done illustrations for a wide spectrum of books, magazines and journals in many language publications, putting him in a league of his own. He has won several national and international accolades for his sharp skill and political perspectives (he recently won the South Korean Jikji prize). Known as the father of calligraphy (Lipi Acchhan) of his literature-rich home state of Keralam, Narayana Bhattathiri has been a consistent co-traveler of the progressive movement in South Asia too.

Ma’din Academy’s Calligraphy Exhibition Commences with Grand Inauguration

News • January 20th, 2024 • 3 mins

On January 20, Ma’din Academy proudly launched a captivating calligraphy exhibition in Malappuram. The event, spanning two days, was inaugurated by international calligrapher Mukhtar Ahmed and Malayalam calligraphy luminary Narayana Bhattathiri. Presiding over the function was Sayyid Ibrahimul Khaleel Al Bukhari, the esteemed chairman of Madin Academy.

Renowned Turkish Calligraphy Master Abdurrahim Kahya Inspires Students During Visit to Kerala

News • January 3rd, 2024 • 7 mins

Turkish calligraphy master Abdurrahim Kahya visited Kerala, imparting his profound knowledge and unparalleled skills to our students. He showcased his artworks and explained the stories and technical details behind them. He encouraged the artistic talents of the students and provided valuable guidance for their future endeavors. His classes enhanced the students’ knowledge and proficiency, addressing their queries about calligraphy. This visit served as an inspiration to the students, giving new direction to their future artistic pursuits.

Master Calligrapher Muqtar Ahmad Inspires Students at Ma’din Academy, Kerala

News • July 3rd, 2023 • 2 mins

Renowned master calligrapher Muqtar Ahmad Sir visited the Ma’din Academy in Kerala. During his visit to the Calligraphy Center, he generously shared his knowledge with the students, offering guidance and advice to help develop their perspectives and future outlooks. The session took place in the library, emphasizing the importance of art and fostering a sense of uniqueness among the students. His presence and encouragement were greatly beneficial, inspiring both enthusiasm and growth in the art of calligraphy.
